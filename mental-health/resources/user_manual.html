ME User Manual

Introduction

'Time Me' times the amount of time the user has been studying, and reminds them to take a break after a study session has gone on too long. Drag the pointer or click anywhere on the timer to choose the length of each study session.

‘Calm Me’ is for the anxious student. It provides breathing exercises, as well as audio recordings for guided meditation.

‘Help Me’ will provide study tips as well as links to various helpful websites including Desmos, Symbolab, Sparknotes, and Quizlet.

‘Distract Me’ is here to provide your brain an entertaining activity that takes the user out of the overpowering stress of homework. Everyone needs a break, right?

'Music': Provides calming music and soothing nature sounds for studying.
